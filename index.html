About Us

Marigold Cinema's is a creative studio and YouTube channel focused on bringing original indie games and web series to life. We're passionate about storytelling, world-building, and exploring new ideas through animation, gameplay, and cinematic content.

Our projects are made by indie creators, for indie fans — whether it's a short web series or a full game experience, we put care into every detail. We're always experimenting, collaborating, and pushing our limits to make something unique.

If you're into fresh, independent content with personality, you're in the right place.
